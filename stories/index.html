We survived our first big fight as an LDR.

My gf (we’ve been best friends for ~5 years) and I are now dating for 3 years. I moved to Australia and and we’ve been in a LDR for around 4 months. I’ve gone back to visit once since then so I got to spend time with her as well, though only for a week. While we’re around 5,200km apart, there’s no time difference so this works for us.

Not getting into the details, but my gf and I had to work through our first huge hurdle today. It’s been a day full of emotions and anxiety, but it gave us the opportunity to really solidify our feelings for each other and that we want a future together. Even with the distance we CAN work through some really tough things. It’s scary, exciting, comforting, all at once. I’m so grateful for her.
