PROCESS

beginning

we are long distance,
our history is more than the days spent together,
the trajectory of our story has been proven in days past and now,
we will meet again, closer than before.
remember: the stars aligned so that I could love you.

The intention of we are long distance, is to create normalcy and positivity around long distance relationships (LDR’s), in order to change peoples’ perspective on the perseverance of LDR’s in a new global era. People need to know that LDR’s can be successful and beautiful.

This is important because people are missing out on opportunities around the world – both career-wise and romantically – for fear of the distance.

we are long distance, consists of three projects that showcase different facets of LDR’s. The website (prototype) aims to unveil the LDR community and encourages a sense of normalcy and positivity. The calendar is a wall installation that validates the time LDR’s spend geographically apart. The book highlights the memories behind a couple’s journey of being in an LDR. Finally, the takeaway, the postcard, brings together the whole story.

research

The beginning of this project is rooted in deep research of the written word, 2D forms, 3D forms and 4D forms that related to the topic at hand: long distance relationships.

exploration

This stage (concept exploration) was the time to diverge my ideas and brainstorm without limitations. Through the research, the main intention was to convey that LDR’s can be beautiful and succesful. And this could be achieved either by: A) redefining a “normal” relationship, B) establishing that personal connection is stronger than physical proxiity, and/or C) LDR’s are a common occurance and growing.

conception

Now was the time to converge in my design process and decide on a direction. It was at this point - while looking at all the concepts laid out - that there was a change in direction. Previously, the goal was to persuade “outsiders” about the success LDR’s. But the realization that I wanted to create something for people already in LDR’s so that their existing relationship can be successful became a strong drive in decision-making.

development

VISUAL IDENTITY

While the visual identity was considered early in the process, it took a while to focus onto a solid concept. The idea that won out was the one that captured the essence of how universal forces pull people together, no matter the distance. Like a magnetic pull or a gravitational pull, people that are meant to be in love will be in love (despite where they are).

WEBSITE

As a designer with no UI/UX background, trying to create a full fledged website was a challenge. The truest difficulty was in creating the ability for new users to make an account: the backend work required for this function took too long for just 2 people. However, the experience of extensive wireframing was a great eye opener to the world of UI/UX.

POSTCARD

The takeaway - the post card - ended up being the running thread through the whole of this project. With three separate projects, the postcard ties these projects together by placing each project into each correlating section of the poem.
