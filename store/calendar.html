days together calendar

The days together calendar visualizes the time an LDR couple spends geographically together and apart over the course of a year. Closed, the calendar is compressed in an accordian-style fold; the time here reflect the days a couple spent physically together. However, when the tab is opened to display the full year, the true length and history of the relationship is revealed in a bright gradient. The days together calendar is a great way to reflect and showcase the strength of your relationship!
